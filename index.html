My vision

I believe everyone in the world can code. Some code for money, others may just for the fun, yet we're still coding. Personally, I aim to perhaps make the digital world a better place for some people. If even one person in the world appreciates my work, that's a win in my book.

My goals

I aspire to be a software engineer in the future. I believe I am capable of becoming what I want, if I want it bad enough. I may be young, but having a headstart is always good. Right now, I hope to better my web development skills.

My view

I view programming as art. I believe people can express themselves through code as much as they can do so with paintings or poems. I believe in equality in all aspects and I wish for a united Earth that has clear goals ahead, where people thrive and live in peace.
